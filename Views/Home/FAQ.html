Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay for my accommodation?
Payments can be made online through the BV Housing platform using credit/debit cards or bank transfer. Students are provided with a secure portal to track payment status and receipts.
Can I visit the accommodation before moving in?
Yes, guided viewings can be scheduled in advance. Virtual tours are also available for students who cannot visit in person.
What is the process for extending my stay?
Students can request an extension through email communication. Extensions are subject to availability and must be confirmed before the current lease ends.
Are there any house rules or community guidelines?
Yes, all accommodations have a set of guidelines to ensure safety, cleanliness, and respectful living. These include quiet hours, visitor policies, and maintenance procedures.
What happens if I need to leave early?
Students who wish to terminate their stay early must submit a email request. Terms and conditions regarding notice periods and potential fees will apply.
Is Wi-Fi included?
Most accommodations include high-speed Wi-Fi as part of the rent. Internet availability is specified in each property listing.
Who can I contact in case of an emergency?
Each property has an emergency contact available 24/7. Students can also contact the BV Housing support team through the platform for urgent issues.
